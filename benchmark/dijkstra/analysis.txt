## Dijkstra 


TARGET_INDEX:
	i, j, size, vertex_set_size, min, u

TARGET_TYPE:
	dist[size]
	vertex_set[size]
	prev[size]
	a[size, size]

Total Memory > (#TARGET_INDEX * #b) + (size * #b) + (size + #b) + (size * #b) + 
									+ (size * size * #b)

(#TARGET_INDEX * #b) + [3 * (size * #b)] + (size^2 * #b) < 128

8051:		128 byte RAM
Atmega128: 	4Kbytes = 4000
